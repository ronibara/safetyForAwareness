This website exists to call attention to the problems that are neglected around U.S. and the world . These problems continue to persist at the expense of millions of individuals whose voices are almost never heard . Awarness For The Neglected was made to be an additional voice to the millions that go unheard while also providing a place for those unaware or uninformed about issues and problems plauging the country can gain insight information.



Human Trafficking

Every year, millions of men, women, and children are trafficked worldwide – including right here in the United States so much so that January is recognized as Human Trafficking awareness month.

Poverty

In 2018, 38.1 million people in the U.S. lived in poverty. That number only continues to grow each year and strikes all demographics differently.
